Esvelt, K., Mali, P., Braff, J., Moosburner, M., Yaung, S. and Church, G., 2013. Orthogonal Cas9 proteins for RNA-guided gene regulation and editing. Nature Methods, 10(11), pp.1116-1121.

In this publication, Esvelt and colleagues characterize the function and orthogonality of 4 Cas9 proteins and their respective sgRNAs. These are tested for their cleavage and binding efficiencies in bacteria, along with gene editing and activation capabilities in human cells.
