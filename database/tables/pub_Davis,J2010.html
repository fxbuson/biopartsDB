Davis, J., Rubin, A. and Sauer, R., 2010. Design, construction and characterization of a set of insulated bacterial promoters. Nucleic Acids Research, 39(3), pp.1131-1141.

In this publication, a series of constitutive promoters are characterized, derived from the promoter pro D. Although the authors normalized the data to the activity of BBa_J23101, we took the liberty to re-normalize it by BBa_J23100, also measured in the same study, for these numbers to be consistent with the caracterization of other constitutive promoters in the database.
